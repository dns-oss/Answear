1. Executive Summary / Streszczenie Wykonawcze

English: The 15% discount on branded products would reduce revenue by 3,389,222 PLN (a 15.0% reduction from the 22.59M PLN baseline) while dramatically improving the competitive position from a 33.3% to 64.4% win rate across 423,900 competitor comparisons. The discount creates a powerful competitive positioning but requires volume growth of >15% to maintain current revenue levels.
